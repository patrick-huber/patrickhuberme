Fair Food Finder

The Minnesota State Fair, was cancelled in 2020, but that didn't stop hungry Minnesotans from wanting a taste of their favorite State Fair foods.

I designed and developed the Fair Food Finder web app to help people find State Fair food vendors open around them and see their hours and details. It reached 11,000 unique users with over 150,000 food stands viewed in the first week of launch.
