Chanl Health

Research Wireframing Prototyping UI Design Coding

Chanl Health is a SaaS platform that scales virtual cardiac rehabilitation for hospitals and patients. I led the product design and development of a patient mobile app and clinician dashboard to deliver daily rehab plans, track progress, and provide actionable insights.

Through clinician and patient research, wireframing, prototyping, and UI design, we created an accessible, motivating, and clinically rigorous platform. Outcomes included a 200% increase in patient enrollment, 3× higher engagement, and 2.5 hours per patient saved for clinicians—demonstrating how thoughtful design can align usability with clinical impact.
